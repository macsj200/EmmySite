Experience

Billie Siddoway has trial and dispute resolution experience in many industries, including agriculture, automotive, legal services, software, analytics, real estate, manufacturing, advertising, hospitality, technology, medical devices, healthcare, and direct sales. Siddoway handles claims for clients of every size, from Fortune 500 companies to small business and individuals involved in business disputes. She has handled contract disputes, antitrust claims, trade secret misappropriation, non-compete and non-solicitation covenants, copyright and trademark claims, cybersquatting, competitive issues and business torts.

Career

Siddoway began the Siddoway Law Office in 2012 as part of a relocation to her home state of Idaho. She was with the firm of Jones Waldo Holbrook & McDonough from 1999 to 2012 and a shareholder, associate, clerk and law librarian. Prior to law school, Billie worked for the Governor of Idaho.

Other

Siddoway was born in St. Anthony, Idaho and attended West Jefferson High School. While growing up, she worked on the family sheep ranch and at the local feed store. Siddoway lives in Victor, Idaho.
